Frequently asked questions

Learn how TK Phones evaluates devices, pays out, and keeps your data safe.

How do you determine the integrity rating?

Our algorithm scores five checkpoints (screen, battery, water exposure, carrier lock, and storage). Each answer adds to a composite score which maps to an integrity rating from 1–4.

Can I edit pricing for each integrity score?

Yes. Admins can sign in via Supabase auth and adjust the buyback prices for each model and quality tier directly from the admin dashboard.

What happens after I save a quote?

When you click “Save quote”, we store the offer details in Supabase. Your operations team can review saved quotes and arrange pickup or shipping instructions.

Do you buy damaged phones?

Yes. Even phones with cracked screens or water damage can receive an Integrity 1 offer so we can responsibly recycle components.

Is my information secure?

All authentication is handled by Supabase. We follow principle of least privilege and only store the answers needed to evaluate the device.
