The Integrity Index™ explained

Our proprietary index evaluates four core vectors to arrive at a 1-4 rating. Each rating tier maps to an adjustable buyback price that our admins can fine-tune.

A

Authenticity

We verify IMEI, carrier status, and activation locks to protect against fraud and ensure every phone can be resold compliantly.

B

Battery wellness

Battery diagnostics capture cycle count, health percentage, and charging behavior to forecast future performance.

C

Cosmetic precision

From micro-scratches to frame dents, our technicians document every detail to ensure listings exceed marketplace expectations.

D

Diagnostics

We run 45+ hardware and software tests, from Face ID to audio to 5G connectivity, to confirm absolute performance.

Dynamic pricing you control

TK Phones links each Integrity rating to a configurable buyback price. Administrators can log in, update payouts, and instantly refresh quotes in the guided quiz.

Editable quality tiers

Adjust payouts for ratings 1-4 anytime. Changes cascade to every customer quote and get logged for compliance.

Real-time offer engine

The guided quiz applies your pricing immediately, factoring in storage multipliers to deliver competitive—but profitable—offers.
