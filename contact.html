Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions from TK Phones customers.

How fast will I get paid?

Instant payouts are available after device grading is finalized—typically within 2 hours of arrival or concierge pickup.

Do you accept Android devices?

Absolutely. While we specialize in flagship phones, we purchase premium Android models from Samsung, Google, OnePlus, and more.

What if my phone is activation locked?

We can help remove locks where possible. Locked devices default to Integrity rating 1 and receive diagnostic assistance before offers are confirmed.

Can I adjust my offer?

Offers adjust automatically if your Integrity answers change. Our concierge will review any discrepancies and re-evaluate pricing in real-time.
