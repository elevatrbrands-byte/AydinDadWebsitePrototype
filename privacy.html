Privacy policy

We collect only the information needed to evaluate your device and complete the trade-in.

Information we collect

When you request a quote we collect the device model, your answers to condition questions, and optional contact details you choose to share with us. If you create an account we also store your email address for authentication.

How we use information

We use your responses to calculate integrity scores and buyback offers. Saved quotes help our team follow up to arrange payments or logistics. We never sell personal data to third parties.

Security

Authentication and data storage are handled by Supabase. Access to administrative features is limited to approved TK Phones staff.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, email privacy@tkphones.example.
