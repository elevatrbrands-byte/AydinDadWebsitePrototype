Why TK Phones?

  • Transparent pricing. Device integrity is scored on a 1–4 scale and mapped to admin-defined offers.
  • Live inventory. Admins can publish and retire models with one click.
  • Instant records. Every quote is saved to Supabase so your team can follow up fast.

How it works

  1. Choose your device model.
  2. Tell us about its condition — screen, battery, water damage, carrier, and storage.
  3. We score integrity from 1 (unusable) to 4 (excellent) and calculate the buyback price.
  4. Lock in your quote and ship or schedule pickup.
